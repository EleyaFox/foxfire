World

The story takes place in a strongly Japanese-themed world, driving inspiration from Japanese nature, folklore, culture and architecture. The characters traverse vibrant meadows, lush forests and grand mountain ranges through the story, yet no humans can be found - even while old buildings litter the landscape and rice paddies line the mountainsides. It is an alternate dimension where spirits and animals take the stage and life is intrinsically bound with the mystical. Each level represents a part of the four seasons, beginning from the budding leaves and cherry blossoms of early spring and ending in the cold and harsh darkness of late winter.
