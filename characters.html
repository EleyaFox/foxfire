Characters

Kitsu

The main character in the game is named as Kitsu, which comes from Japanese term “kitsune”, meaning simply “a fox”. Its tails, foxfires and all of her talents all come from old Japanese folklores. Kitsu’s appearance is designed by the team. Kitsu looks a fennec like fox with big ears, golden fur and comparably little body. Kitsu is a female, and she has kind but playful personality. Unlike other animals in the game, she cannot talk before she has acquired her missing foxfires and her tails back from the evil villain, Tachi the Mage. After her year lasting journey, which we see in the game, Kitsu encounters Tachi the Mage and battles him to get her lost foxfires back to acquire her last lost tail. With 9 tails, which is the maximum amount of tails to a kitsune, she can finally transform to beautiful woman, and talk. The first things she says is to thank her friends who helped her with her long journey.

Tachi the Mage

The story’s main villain is a Japanese weasel called Tachi the Mage, who has lived over 900 years and has developed himself powerful yokai skills. Tachi was jealous of Kitsu’s tails and skills so he used his own skills to steal main character Kitsu’s foxfires, what he knew was the source of his power. Tachi’s goal is to rule the animal world with his right hand, Beef the Bear. Since they saw the main protagonist as a threat, Tachi and his lieutenants scattered the foxfires all over the country and placed guardians near them so Kitsu could never get them back. Tachi can change forms between human monk and weasel. Tachi is a vile and a wicked person and he is always scheming. He thinks that everyone should follow only his rules or face a painful and gruesome death!
