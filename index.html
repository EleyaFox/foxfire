About

This website is about a school project. Our team is three it-students and we study at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences.
It's our first year and third period "Games and mobiles". Our team is: Ilse Heino (me), Anu Kakko and Taru Liukkonen.

Foxfire is a 2D sidescroller platformer game. Our goal was to create a beautiful game with cute and sympathic characters and also exiting story on it. The game will be eight levels and work is in progress. Foxfire will be availabe for Windows, Android and iOS.
